About

Greetings! I'm KT, a passionate and dedicated software engineering student with a lifelong fascination for computers and complex algorithms. From the early days of my childhood, I've been captivated by the intricate world of technology, and that curiosity has evolved into a driving force in my journey as a full-stack Java software engineer.

My path to this point has been an interesting one, marked by twists and turns that have ultimately fueled my commitment to this field. While my journey began with bits and pieces of coding during my high school days, it wasn't until the pandemic disrupted my sprouting career in hairdressing that I truly immersed myself in the world of software engineering. This pivotal moment compelled me to seize the opportunity and embark on a serious exploration of this captivating domain.

Presently, I'm pursuing a double major in Software Engineering and Computer Science at Western Governors University, a testament to my dedication to honing my skills and knowledge. Through this formal education, I've gained a solid foundation that complements the self-study I've undertaken over the years.

My expertise extends beyond the classroom, as I've delved into various projects that highlight my passion for full-stack Java development. While some of these projects are still in the development phase, they reflect my commitment to crafting efficient and innovative solutions.

One area that holds a special place in my heart is web accessibility. While I understand that fresh graduates are continually evolving, I'm particularly passionate about making digital experiences inclusive for all users. I firmly believe that technology should empower everyone, regardless of their abilities.

My journey has included participation in game jams, project contributions, and hackathons, experiences that have fostered my collaborative spirit and problem-solving acumen. I approach each challenge by dissecting its core elements, embracing the "Who What Where Why and How" to unravel its complexity.

As I move forward, I remain steadfast in my commitment to coding practices that prioritize legibility, accessibility, and clear communication. I'm excited to contribute my unique perspective and enthusiasm to the dynamic world of software engineering.

I'm genuinely proud of the winding path that has led me here, and I'm excited to embrace the future's opportunities and challenges with open arms. Join me as we journey into the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
